          On the teams screen, swipe left on a team's name and tap on the pencil icon (in the blue block).

          You can rename the team or changes its player limit - but you cannot change its guild.

          On the teams screen, swipe left on a team's name and tap on the trash icon (in the red block).

          Tap the "Delete" option in the confirmation dialogue box that appears.

          On the team players screen, swipe left on a player's name and tap on the trash icon (in the red block).

          Tap the "Delete" option in the confirmation dialogue box that appears.

          All the card images, plots, and guides are included within the app itself; you do not need to have an Internet connection to use the app.

          Your teams are stored within the app; they remain when you close the app or turn off your device.

          If you delete the app, all data associated with the app is also deleted (including your teams).

          On the teams screen, swipe left on a team's name and tap on the share icon (in the green block).

          This will open a compose email screen with the team's details in; you'll need to have an Internet connection to send the email.

          In the Misc. section, tap on Settings.

          Toggling the "Create HTML emails when sharing teams" setting off will generate plain text emails when sharing a team.

          You cannot sync teams between different devices currently - but this is something we are working on as part of a future update.

          The guide documents included in the app are PDFs.

          Selecting a guide from the app will open it in a basic PDF reader.

          If you need more advanced features (such as bookmarking or text search), you can open a guide document in a different PDF reader (such as iBooks) by clicking the share icon at the top right of the basic PDF reader.
